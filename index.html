Historical Places

 

Sigiriya Rock Fortress 

Sigiriya or Sinhagiri is an ancient rock fortress located in the northern Matale District near the town of Dambulla in the Central Province, Sri Lanka. 

Visit 
Temple of Tooth Relic 

The Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic or Sri Dalada Maligawa, is a Buddhist temple in Kandy, Sri Lanka. It is located in the royal palace complex of the former Kingdom of Kandy, which houses the relic of the tooth of the Buddha. 

Visit 
 
Polonnaruwa Gal Viharaya 

The Gal Vihar and known originally as the Uttararama is a rock temple of the Buddha situated in the ancient city Polonnaruwa. 

Visit 
 

 

Adam's Peak 

Adam's Peak or Śrī Pāda is a 2,243 m (7,359 ft) tall conical sacred mountain located in central Sri Lanka.It is well known for the "Śrī Pāda", i.e., "sacred footprint", a 1.8 m (5 ft 11 in) rock formation near the summit..

Visit 
 
Buduruwagala 

Buduruwagala is an ancient buddhist temple in Sri Lanka. The complex consists of seven statues and belongs to the Mahayana school of thought.

Visit 
 
Sri Maha Bodhiya 

Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi is a historical sacred bo tree (Ficus religiosa) in the Mahamewuna Garden in historical city of Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka.

Visit 
 

 

 

Nature & Wild Life

 

Nine Arche Bridge 

The Nine Arches Bridge also called the Bridge in the Sky, is a viaduct bridge in Sri Lanka. It is one of the best examples of colonial-era railway construction in the country.

Peradeniya Bottanical Garaden 

Royal Botanic Gardens, Peradeniya are about 5.5 km to the west of the city of Kandy in the Central Province of Sri Lanka. In 2016.

Meemure 

Meemure is a village with a population of about 420 people living in it. It is located near the border between Kandy District and Matale District in the Knuckles Mountain Range.  

Visit 
 
Wilpattu National Park 

Wilpattu National Park is a national park in Sri Lanka. The unique feature of this park is the existence of "Willus" (natural lakes) – natural, sand-rimmed water basins or depressions that fill with rainwater. 

Visit 
 

 

Beach Sides 

 

Unawatuna Beach 

Unawatuna is a coastal town in Galle district of Sri Lanka. Unawatuna is a major tourist attraction in Sri Lanka and known for its beach and corals. 

Visit 
 
Mirissa Beach 

Mirissa is a small town on the south coast of Sri Lanka, located in the Matara District of the Southern Province.  

Visit 
 
Marble Beach 

Marble Beach is a beautiful beach located in Trincomalee, Sri Lanka. It is known for its white sand, clear waters, and stunning views of the Indian Ocean. The beach is also home to a number of coral reefs, making it a popular spot for snorkeling and diving.  

Visit 
 

 

Talalla Beach 

Talalla Beach is a beautiful beach located in the south of Sri Lanka. It is known for its golden sand, clear waters, and laid-back atmosphere. The beach is a popular spot for swimming, sunbathing, surfing, and stand-up paddleboarding. 

Visit 
 
Hikkaduwa Coral Reef 

Hikkaduwa is a coastal town in Galle district of Sri Lanka. Hikkaduwa is a major tourist attraction in Sri Lanka and is known for its beaches and corals. It is located in the Southern Province
